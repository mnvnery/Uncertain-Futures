Phase 2 of the project will begin in September 2022 and is centred around three main goals:

  • Analysis

  • Exhibiting

  • Activation

The Analysis phase of the project involves the co-analysis of the interviews by the research team which includes the researchers from the University of Manchester and Manchester Metropolitan University, as well members of the Advisory Group namely: Atiha Chaudry, Rohina Ghafoor, Marie Greenhalgh and Tendayi Madzunzu. The analysis of the interviews will culminate in a report which is due to be launched in March 2023.


The Exhibiting phase of the project involves a newly designed exhibit in Manchester Art Gallery, Gallery 8 to reflect the ever-changing nature of the project. Part of the exhibition includes a timeline of the significant moments of the project as well as identifying important research themes and moments arising from the project.


The Activation phase of the project involves using the outcomes of the research to impact change in our communities in Manchester. This involves a number of events and public engagement opportunities as well as learning opportunities. All of our activation events will be advertised on the Events section of our website located in the footer.
